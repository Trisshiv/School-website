Academics

  • Class Details

    The school has classes from K.G to XII. The school follows the next education CBSE syllabus and has an impressive teacher-student ratio of 1:20 with an upper limit of 34 in each class.



  • Methods of Teaching

    A student centered style of teaching instills confidence, initiative, creativity, development of critical and analytical skills. Students learn through teacher instruction and group activities. Communicative English classes, Personality Development classes, workshops, seminars, presentations and field trips also form an integral part of learning.



  • The Faculty

    * A team of dedicated and trained faculty contributes to the learning process through effective and innovative teaching methods.

    * Teachers keep abreast of the teaching and learning trends by attending workshops organized in-house and district and state level seminars and conferences.

    * The faculties are empowered with technology training that helps in integration of technology with teaching.
